Limitations ​

- Non Volumetric Clouds ​

For performance reasons, Fast Sky is not a volumetric solution, so lighting isn't entirely physically accurate. However, its approximations produce good results.

- No Clouds Flythrough Support ​

Since Fast Sky is a non-volumetric background shader, flying through clouds is not possible.
